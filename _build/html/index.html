West Africa Data Analysis Workshop

In today's data-driven world, the ability to harness and analyze data is paramount for effective policymaking. This comprehensive and hands-on course equips students with the essential knowledge and skills to navigate the intricate landscape of data generation, management, analysis, and ethical considerations in policymaking. This course will explore the foundations of data, data pipeline, data formats, and the impact of data protection on data quality. Participants will master techniques for finding, scraping, and integrating data from various sources, including from APIs. Participants will apply principles of effective visualization and gain hands-on experience with tools like Excel, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and GIS. Furthermore, the course will build on topics from intermediate statistics to advanced topics in inference and machine learning. This course empowers participants with a deep and critical understanding of data management, analysis, and ethical considerations, preparing them to make informed decisions and drive meaningful change in their areas of practice.

By: The World Bank Group
